"LLMs don’t break software correctness — they relocate where correctness must be governed."

---

2) Define “correctness” operationally
Add one short definition early:

"Correctness here means: system behavior matches specified intent, across every interpretive boundary."

3) Ground Spec‑Surface Engineering (SSE)
After introducing the name, add a paragraph clarifying what it is not and what it is:

"Spec‑Surface Engineering is not a new development process, framework, or methodology. 
It treats specifications as the governing interface between intent and generated artifacts — 
the surface where correctness must be enforced."
